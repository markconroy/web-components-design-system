Cards are used to display previews of content in a consistent way. Each card can have props for title, teaser text, image, and a link.

  • card-title - The title of the card
  • card-link - The link for the card
  • image-position - The position of the image (top, left, right)
  • image-src - The source of the image
  • image-alt - The alt text for the image
